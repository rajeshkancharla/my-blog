Our Services

Four Things We Actually Build

Discovery

AI Use Case Discovery

A short, focused engagement - typically one to three sessions - to map where AI can genuinely reduce friction in your business. We look at your current workflows, identify the repetitive and time-consuming tasks, and give you a clear, honest picture of what's worth building and what isn't.

The output is a concrete recommendation, not a generic strategy document. You walk away knowing exactly what to build first and why.

  • Workflow and process review
  • Prioritised list of practical AI opportunities
  • Honest assessment of effort vs. impact
  • No commitment required beyond the discovery itself
Suitable for: Any SME exploring AI for the first time
Build

Custom AI Assistants & Agents

We design and build AI agents tailored to your business - not off-the-shelf chatbots, but purpose-built assistants that connect to the tools you already use and handle the tasks that are currently eating your team's time.

We've built agents that manage email and calendar, chase documents, answer questions about uploaded files, handle WhatsApp messages, and coordinate tasks across Google and Microsoft environments. We build what your business actually needs, not what a vendor's demo shows.

  • Agents connected to email, calendar, and task tools
  • Voice and WhatsApp interfaces where useful
  • Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 integration
  • Deployed on cloud infrastructure you control
Suitable for: Professional services, property, finance, operations
Knowledge

Document Intelligence

If your business runs on documents - contracts, statements, reports, client files - there's usually a better way to work with them than manually reading through each one. We build systems that let you search, query, and extract information from your document library using plain language.

Ask a question, get the answer and the source. Works across PDFs, Word documents, scanned files, and mixed-format archives. Built using vector search and retrieval-augmented generation - the same approach used in enterprise document management tools, right-sized for an SME.

  • Natural language search across your document library
  • Supports PDFs, DOCX, scanned documents, and images
  • Answers grounded in your actual documents, not general AI knowledge
  • Secure - your documents stay within your environment
Suitable for: Law firms, accountants, conveyancers, financial brokers
Foundation

Data Pipeline & Integration

AI only works well when the data feeding it is clean, structured, and reliable. Many businesses have data scattered across spreadsheets, systems that don't talk to each other, and manual processes bridging the gaps. Before building anything clever on top, the foundations need to be right.

We build straightforward data pipelines that consolidate, clean, and structure your data - whether that's for feeding an AI system, powering a dashboard, or simply giving you a single source of truth you can trust.

  • Data consolidation from multiple sources and systems
  • Automated pipelines that replace manual data handling
  • Cloud-based storage and processing
  • Built as a prerequisite for AI, or as a standalone improvement
Suitable for: Any business where data is spread across systems
