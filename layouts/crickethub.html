What Is CricketHub?

Multi-Game Squad Planning Made Simple

CricketHub is built for cricket organisers who need to manage player assignments across several games happening at the same time - club weekends, tournaments, round-robins, or any setup where the same squad is split across multiple matches.

Enter your squad once, configure each game's batting order and bowling lineup, then manage who is batting, who is bowling, and who is sitting out - all on a single interactive board. Changes update instantly across all games.

It's a Progressive Web App, so it installs to your home screen like a native app, works fully offline after the first load, and saves your team plan automatically between sessions. Share your full lineup with anyone via a single URL.

How It Works

From Squad to Full Lineup in Four Steps

CricketHub guides you through setup to a shareable, exportable team plan.

1

Configure Your Squad & Games

Set the squad size (6–15 players), the number of batting positions (6–11), how many bowlers you need, and the number of concurrent games (up to 10). Each game can also be given a date and a match format - from 5 Overs all the way through to a 5 Day Match.

2

Enter Your Players

Paste or type your player names into the squad text area, one per line. CricketHub validates the list matches your configured squad size and then loads each player as an assignable tile on the planning board.

3

Assign Players to Games

Drag player tiles from the squad pool into each game column - place them in numbered batting positions, mark them as "Not Bowling" (bats only), or assign them to "Not Batting" (bowls only). On mobile, tap to select and tap again to assign. The app enforces assignment rules automatically.

4

Export, Share & Report

Export a print-ready PDF team sheet for all games, or generate an analytics report covering batting position matrices, per-game availability summaries, and absence statistics across the full tournament. Share your entire lineup with anyone via a single URL - no account required on either end.

Features

Everything a Cricket Organiser Needs

Built to handle real-world tournament and club weekend planning - here's what CricketHub does out of the box.

🎮

Multi-Game Planning Board

Manage up to 10 concurrent games from a single view. Each game column shows its batting order, not-bowling, and not-batting sections side by side, so you can see the full picture at a glance and balance your squad across matches.

🖱️

Drag-and-Drop Assignment

Drag players from the squad pool into any game column and position. Re-arrange the batting order by dragging within a column. On mobile and tablets, use tap-to-select and tap-to-place for a fully touch-friendly experience.

🏏

Batting & Bowling Roles

Assign each player one of three roles per game: a numbered batting position, "Not Bowling" (bats but doesn't bowl), or "Not Batting" (bowls only). The app enforces role logic - a not-batting player can't appear in the batting order.

📄

PDF Team Sheet Export

Generate a clean, print-ready PDF covering all your games in one go. Each game column shows the full batting lineup with numbered positions, match format, and date - ready to hand out on the day or share digitally.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Generate a detailed analytics PDF with three views: a batting position matrix showing where each player batted across all games, per-game availability summaries, and absence statistics highlighting who missed which matches and how often.

🔗

Share via URL

Share your entire team plan - squad, assignments, and game metadata - as a single URL. The full state is encoded directly into the link so anyone can open it and see the lineup with no account needed on either side.

📱

Progressive Web App

Install CricketHub to your iPhone or Android home screen for a native app feel - no App Store required. The app works fully offline after the first load, and your team plan is saved automatically between sessions via local storage.

📅

Game Metadata

Attach a date and match format to each game - covering everything from a 5-over blast to a full 5 Day Match. Metadata appears on exported team sheets so your printouts are always clearly labelled.

🔓

No Account Needed

CricketHub is fully open - no sign-up, no login, no subscription. Open the link, build your team, export and share. Your data lives on your device and travels via URL - nothing is stored on any server.
