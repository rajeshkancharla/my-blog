## Who We Are

Dataverse is a Data & AI Engineering consultancy based in Sydney, Australia.
We specialise in building intelligent, scalable data solutions that solve real-world business problems.

## What We Do

We work at the intersection of modern data engineering and artificial intelligence — helping organisations move from raw data to actionable intelligence.

Our work spans:

- **Data Pipeline Architecture** — scalable, reliable, cloud-native pipelines
- **AI Agent Development** — intelligent automation using LangChain and OpenAI
- **Cloud Infrastructure** — Google Cloud, Cloud Run, and serverless architectures
- **Data Platform Modernisation** — migrating legacy systems to modern stacks

## Our Approach

We believe in building production-grade systems from day one. Every solution we deliver is designed for reliability, observability, and scale.
