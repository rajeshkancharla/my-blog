What Is Jarvis?

An AI Agent That Works the Way You Do

Jarvis isn't a chatbot that answers generic questions. It's a production-grade AI agent that connects directly to your inbox, calendar, documents, and business tools - then reasons, plans, and acts on your behalf.

Ask it to draft a client email, check your schedule, find a document, follow up on an outstanding invoice, or summarise a contract. Jarvis handles it - and it remembers the context of your conversation so you don't have to repeat yourself.

Built on OpenAI's latest models and a battle-tested agentic framework, Jarvis has been running in production as a personal assistant and is now being shaped into a product purpose-built for small and medium businesses.

Core Capabilities

Everything It Can Handle For You

Jarvis is a multi-tool AI agent. Here's what it does out of the box.

📧

Email - Read, Draft & Send

Search, read, and reply to Gmail threads. Draft client emails in your voice. Process PDF and Word attachments and extract key information. Archive, label, and organise your inbox on command.

📅

Calendar & Scheduling

View today's agenda, create meetings with attendees, set reminders, and update or cancel events - all via natural language. Handles timezone conversions and avoids double-booking automatically.

📄

Document Intelligence

Upload contracts, reports, or statements and ask Jarvis questions about them. Reads PDFs and Word documents - including scanned files via OCR - and gives precise, grounded answers from the content.

✅

Tasks & To-Dos

Add, view, and complete tasks across Google Tasks lists. Say "remind me to follow up with the Hendersons on Friday" and Jarvis creates the reminder - no tapping around in an app required.

🗂️

Google Drive

Upload files to specific Drive folders, organise attachments by sender or client, and resolve nested folder structures automatically. Pairs with email to file incoming documents with zero manual effort.

🌐

Web Search & Research

Search the web, summarise a URL, or compile a structured report on any topic - with source citations. Useful for market research, competitor checks, or quickly answering client questions.

🗺️

Navigation & Commute

Get real-time travel times between any two locations accounting for live traffic. Knows your home and work addresses and offers toll vs. non-toll route options when relevant.

🎙️

Voice In, Voice Out

Hold a button and speak. Jarvis transcribes your message, thinks, and responds - in text or as a voice note. Works on the web app and on WhatsApp, so you can use it hands-free while between meetings.

🧠

Agentic Reasoning

Jarvis doesn't just answer - it reasons across multiple steps. Ask it to "find the email from the accountant, pull out the figures, and add a reminder to review them Thursday" and it will, without prompting each step individually.
